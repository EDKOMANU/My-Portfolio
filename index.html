Professional Journey and Research

Edward is a passionate demographer and data scientist with expertise in statistical analysis, machine learning, and demographic research. They are currently developing an R package called 'Dem', which focuses on demographic analysis, including life tables, population projections, and fertility modeling.

Currently, Edward works at the Ghana Statistical Service, leading efforts in the Governance, Peace, and Security session, exploring statistical programs and stakeholder collaborations to enhance data-driven policymaking. Their work involves designing methodologies, conducting advanced data analysis, and ensuring that statistical outputs support decision-making at national and international levels.

Beyond their professional role, Edward is deeply interested in household characteristics and multidimensional poverty, with a research focus on living standards. Their analytical skills extend to survey data analysis, spatial data visualization, and machine learning, particularly in predicting employee turnover and studying economic indicators like inflation and exchange rates.
